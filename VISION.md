# Product Vision

Shoptet Address Autocomplete should make checkout and company checkout setup feel fast, trustworthy, and effortless for Czech and Slovak merchants and their customers.

The product should help shoppers complete billing and delivery details with minimal typing, fewer errors, and clear confirmation that the entered data is valid. For merchants, it should reduce abandoned orders, bad addresses, and support overhead caused by incorrect company or contact details.

From an operator perspective, the integration should be simple to install, safe to run in production, and easy to understand without reading source code. The expected outcome is a checkout helper that merchants can adopt quickly, trust in daily use, and keep running with low maintenance.
